Our model

Full-cycle developer and operator.

We don't build and sell. We build and operate. Every asset stays on our balance sheet, generating long-term contracted revenue under bankable project finance structures.

01

Develop

Site identification, grid connection studies, environmental permitting, CRE/CENACE registration, technology selection, financial structuring.

02

Build

EPC procurement and supervision, grid connection execution, commissioning and performance testing. Modular design for fast deployment and cost-efficient replication.

03

Operate

O&M management, dispatch optimization, MEM participation where applicable, client reporting, PPA compliance. Assets operated for 20–25 year lifetimes.
